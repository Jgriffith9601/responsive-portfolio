About Me

James Griffith is a Wireless Communications expert. He has worked for large companies such as Verizon Wireless, T-Mobile, to small companies such as Tomorrow Telecom. James has a true passion for the industry and can routinely be found thumbing through related news articles to making bold predictions about the future of wireless.


James started out as a part time mobile phone rep selling devices out of the local Target stores. After drastically increasing sales numbers at his first store he was promoted to wireless manager and was given his own store to run. Now, just a few years later he is the area sales manager for Tomorrow Telecom, and helps manage 22 retail locations across Kansas and Missouri!
